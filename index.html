1. What is Tarot?
Tarot is an ancient divination practice that uses a deck of cards to gain insights and guidance into various aspects of life, including relationships, career, and personal growth. It's a tool for self-reflection and connecting with your intuition.

2. How do your Tarot readings work?
During a Tarot reading, I will use the Tarot cards to tap into your energy and provide you with intuitive insights and guidance. You may ask specific questions or simply request a general reading for a broader perspective.

3. Are Tarot readings accurate?
Tarot readings are subjective and based on the interpretation of the cards. While I strive to provide accurate and meaningful insights, please understand that Tarot is for entertainment purposes only and not meant to replace professional advice.

Tarot does not predict the future with certainty. Instead, it offers guidance and potential outcomes based on the current energies and circumstances. Your actions and choices also play a significant role in shaping your future.
